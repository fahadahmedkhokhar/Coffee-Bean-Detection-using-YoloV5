About Us

Welcome to Bunnah: Redefining Coffee Bean Quality Control with Advanced Computer Vision.

At Bunnah, we're dedicated to enhancing every aspect of your coffee enjoyment. Our state-of-the-art object detection technology meticulously categorizes coffee beans into four distinct classes: Normal, Burnt, Green, and Broken. Through the fusion of sophisticated algorithms and precise visual analysis, we ensure that each bean meets the highest standards of quality and consistency. Join us on a journey where innovation meets tradition, shaping the future of coffee one bean at a time. Discover the art and science behind Bunnah, where every bean tells a story.
